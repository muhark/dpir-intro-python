Method

Primer on AGW21 and SRL

  1. Split corpus into sentences
  2. Apply SRL
  3. Simplify spans labelled as roles ARG0, ARG1 and ARG2
  4. Embed phrases
  5. Cluster embeddings
  6. Label clusters
  7. Retain only complete \(\langle ARG0, V, ARG1 \rangle\) narrative tuples.

Pipeline Example

ORIGINAL: He discovered trying to introduce market reforms into a heavily state controlled economy produces corruption and all sorts of by products.

PARSE: [ARG0: He] [V: discovered] [ARG1: trying to introduce market reforms into a heavily state controlled economy produces corruption and all sorts of by products] .

REDUCED: man discover cost

More BERT more good

  • SRL BERT (Shi et al 2019)
  • Phrase Embedding (Phrase-BERT)
  • SpaCy RoBERTa for POS-tagging, disambiguation, dependency parsing

Labelling Strategy

  • AGW21 lemmas, exclude if >4 tokens
  • Use dependency parsing, identify head noun phrase of role span
  • Keep all tokens direct descendants of head noun phrase
